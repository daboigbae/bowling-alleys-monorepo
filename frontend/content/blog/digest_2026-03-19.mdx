## 🎳 On the Lanes

Rookie Brandon Bonta just made the kind of debut every pro dreams about — [rolling a perfect 300 game to win the 2026 PBA Players Championship](https://www.bowlersmart.com/2026/02/22/rookie-brandon-bonta-rolls-300-to-win-pba-players-championship-2026/). That's how you make a first impression on tour. The kid's got nerves of steel throwing strikes when it matters most.

California squads are making their presence known, with one team [claiming the Classified lead](https://bowl.com/news/california-squad-claims-classified-lead-at-2026-usbc-open-championships) while Oregonians grabbed [early control in Standard Doubles](https://bowl.com/news/oregonians-take-early-lead-in-standard-doubles-at-2026-open-championships).
