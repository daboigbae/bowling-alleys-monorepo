# Top Bowling Alleys in Los Angeles, CA

Los Angeles and the greater metro offer everything from historic spots like Highland Park Bowl to large centers such as Shatto 39 and Bowlero Montebello. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around the City of Angels.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[Los Angeles City Guide](/best-bowling-in-los-angeles)**.

## 1. [Level Up Bowl & Bistro](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/tDcXikdSRlbvJ0oAoVxC)

**Location**: 550 Deep Valley Dr Ste 127, Rolling Hills Estates, CA 90274  
**Rating**: 5.0/5 (21 reviews)  
**Phone**: (310) 377-7700  
**Website**: [levelupbowl.com](http://www.levelupbowl.com/)

Event space, parties, arcade, darts, lounge, cosmic bowling, food, and kid-friendly fun. Open until 9:00 PM Mon–Thu and 10:00 PM Fri–Sun. South Bay / Palos Verdes area.

## 2. [Highland Park Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/XHXgvOpKA2P2rBfTWbaz)

**Location**: 5621 N Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90042  
**Lanes**: 8  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (1,312 reviews)  
**Phone**: (323) 616-2810  
**Website**: [highlandparkbowl.com](http://highlandparkbowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $70/hour, $7 shoe rental  
**Price Level**: $$ (Moderate)

Established in 1927 during prohibition; restored by 1933 Group with original details and refurbished lanes. Billiards, food, bar, and specials. Open until midnight most nights and 2:00 AM Fri–Sat. Northeast LA / Highland Park.

## 3. [Gardena Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/J4dGUcGgMTpmV46hS0JF)

**Location**: 15707 S Vermont Ave, Gardena, CA 90247  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (964 reviews)  
**Phone**: (310) 324-1244  
**Website**: [gardenabowl.com](http://www.gardenabowl.com/)  
**Price Level**: $ (Budget-friendly)

Longtime bowling alley with billiards, arcade, pro shop, lounge, and snack bar. Leagues, bowling specials, open bowling, food, and bar. [Leagues](https://gardenabowl.com/leagues/). Open until 11:00 PM most nights and midnight Saturday. South LA / Gardena.
