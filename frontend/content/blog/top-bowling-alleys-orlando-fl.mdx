- **Primrose Lanes**: Strong rating (4.6); near downtown; sports bar, karaoke, arcade; $38/hr.
- **Boardwalk Bowl**: Most lanes (80) in the Southeast; Brunswick Sync games; leagues and tournaments.
- **Bowlero Altamonte**: Reliable chain option; Altamonte Springs; arcade, pro shop, specials.
- **Aloma Bowl**: 32 lanes in Winter Park; full grill and bar; leagues and corporate events.
- **Oviedo Bowling Center**: 24 lanes; cosmic and glow bowling; Oviedo; specials.
- **AMF Sky Lanes**: 32 lanes on S Orange Blossom Trl; pro shop, arcade, specials.
- **Bowlero Apopka**: Northwest metro; leagues, pro shop; Apopka.
- **Airport Lanes**: 32 lanes near Sanford Airport; casual spot with arcade and billiards.
- **Kings**: Best for I-Drive; elevated food and drinks; bowling, billiards, arcade; $24/hr.
