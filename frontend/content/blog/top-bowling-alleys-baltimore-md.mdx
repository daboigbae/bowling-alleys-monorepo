# Top Bowling Alleys in Baltimore, MD

Baltimore and the surrounding area offer a strong mix of large centers, classic duckpin houses, and upscale bowling near the harbor. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around Charm City.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[Baltimore City Guide](/best-bowling-in-baltimore)**.

## 1. [Crofton Bowling Centre](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/rwNT9I15cIlvlsmwscCc)

**Location**: 2115 Priest Bridge Dr, Crofton, MD 21114  
**Lanes**: 48  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (1,846 reviews)  
**Phone**: (410) 721-2401  
**Website**: [croftonbowl.com](http://www.croftonbowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $73.99/hour

Large bowling complex with 48 lanes, league play, arcade, snack bar, sports bar, and pro shop. Cosmic bowling, leagues, parties, and food. [League bowling](https://croftonbowl.com/league-bowling/). Open until 10:00 PM most nights and 1:00 AM Fri–Sat. Crofton.

## 2. [Mustang Alley's](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/jdCJC9wkxAm4ChMHgitI)

**Location**: 1300 Bank St #2, Baltimore, MD 21231  
**Lanes**: 12  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (926 reviews)  
**Phone**: (410) 522-2695  
**Website**: [mustangalleys.com](http://www.mustangalleys.com/)  
**Pricing**: $60/hour  
**Price Level**: $$$ (Upscale)

**Duckpin bowling** with a chef-inspired menu, full-service bar (craft beer and cocktails), and private event room. Near Fells Point, Little Italy, Harbor East, and Inner Harbor. **Closed Monday and Sunday.** Open Tue–Wed 4:00–10:30 PM, Thu–Fri until 11:30 PM, Sat 12:00–11:30 PM.

## 3. [Parkville Bowling Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/uwiBHE6NkGVMEl6MkWT8)

**Location**: 7607 Harford Rd, Baltimore, MD 21234  
**Lanes**: 26  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (317 reviews)  
**Phone**: (410) 444-6100  
**Website**: [parkvillebowl.com](http://www.parkvillebowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $40/hour, $5 shoe rental

**Duckpin bowling** on 26 lanes. Cosmic and glow bowling, parties, leagues, bar, and food. [Leagues](https://www.parkvillebowl.com/leagues). **Closed Sunday.** Open until 6:00–10:00 PM depending on day. North Baltimore / Parkville.

## 4. [Shake & Bake Family Fun Center](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/UGulIrZpsEEymtYi7KnD)

**Location**: 1601 Pennsylvania Ave, Baltimore, MD 21217  
**Rating**: 4.4/5 (509 reviews)  
**Phone**: (410) 545-1977  
**Website**: [bcrp.baltimorecity.gov/shakeandbake](https://bcrp.baltimorecity.gov/shakeandbake)  
**Pricing**: $30/hour, $3 shoe rental

Baltimore City Recreation and Parks (BCRP). Bowling and roller skating under one roof in the historic Upton community (West Baltimore). “The Bake” since 1982, named after Glenn “Shake & Bake” Doughty. Cosmic and glow bowling, parties. **Closed Monday and Tuesday.**

## 5. [Pinland Bowling Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/rybqJjgJrYJlMVo0WtaK)

**Location**: 10 N Dundalk Ave, Baltimore, MD 21222  
**Rating**: 4.4/5 (169 reviews)  
**Phone**: (443) 437-3318

Established in 1951; new ownership in 2022. **Closed Tuesday.** Open evenings and weekend sessions (check venue for current hours). Dundalk area.

## 6. [Annapolis Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/deVyOQ9NzfPb9wgVL0la)

**Location**: 2057 Generals Hwy, Annapolis, MD 21401  
**Lanes**: 32  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (448 reviews)  
**Phone**: (410) 266-0700  
**Website**: [gobowlingannapolis.com](http://www.gobowlingannapolis.com/)  
**Pricing**: $8.49/game, $39.99/hour, $5.99 shoe rental

Late-night and family-oriented specials, snack bar with comfort food. Glow and cosmic bowling, leagues, parties, food, and bar. [Leagues](https://gobowlingannapolis.com/leagues/). Open until 10:00 PM most nights and 1:00 AM Fri–Sat. Annapolis.

## 7. [Stoneleigh Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/lKrD4rJ5N4iLnzaO6f2y)

**Lanes**: 16  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (336 reviews)  
**Phone**: (410) 377-8115  
**Website**: [stoneleighlanes.com](https://stoneleighlanes.com/)

## 8. [Patterson Pins](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/cVHi9jNrIGgS6tobzD94)

Revival of the iconic Patterson Lanes: two **duckpin** lanes, rotating arcade games, and a video-game-inspired craft cocktail menu. Food, bar, arcade, parties. **Closed Tuesday and Wednesday.** Near Fells Point.

## Planning Your Visit

- **Crofton Bowling Centre**: Most lanes (48) in the list; Crofton; cosmic, leagues, sports bar.
- **Mustang Alley's**: Only upscale duckpin in the city; near Fells Point & Inner Harbor; $$$; closed Mon & Sun.
- **Parkville Bowling Lanes**: 26 duckpin lanes; cosmic, glow, leagues; closed Sun.
- **Shake & Bake**: BCRP; bowling + roller skating; West Baltimore; “The Bake” since 1982; closed Mon–Tue.
- **Pinland Bowling Lanes**: Classic since 1951; Dundalk; closed Tue.
- **Annapolis Bowl**: 32 lanes; Annapolis; late-night & family specials; $8.49/game.
- **Stoneleigh Lanes**: 16 duckpin lanes; karaoke & live bands weekends; $22/hr; closed Mon.
- **Patterson Pins**: Two duckpin lanes, arcade, craft cocktails; Fells Point area; closed Tue–Wed.
- **AMF Southdale**: 32 lanes; Pasadena; reserve online.
- **AMF Perry Hall**: 48 lanes; Nottingham; specials and leagues.

Baltimore’s duckpin scene is a standout—Mustang Alley's, Parkville, Stoneleigh, and Patterson Pins all offer it. For full details, pricing, and hours, use our **[Baltimore City Guide](/best-bowling-in-baltimore)**.
