# Top Bowling Alleys in St Paul, MN

St Paul, Minneapolis, and the greater Twin Cities offer a strong mix of entertainment centers, neighborhood alleys, and iconic spots like Bryant Lake Bowl and Memory Lanes. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around St Paul.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[St Paul / Twin Cities City Guide](/best-bowling-in-st-paul)**.

## 1. [PINZ](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/aiF6hm9x3M4FqlGxhmiX)

**Location**: 7520 32nd St N, Oakdale, MN 55128  
**Lanes**: 34  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (4,119 reviews)  
**Phone**: (651) 770-8000  
**Website**: [tripleshift.com/pinz](https://tripleshift.com/pinz/)  
**Pricing**: $45.99/hour, $5.99 shoe rental

Bowl, share laughs, and enjoy a full menu of food and drinks. Cosmic and glow bowling, laser tag, arcade, leagues, tournaments, and parties. [Leagues](https://tripleshift.com/pinz/leagues/). Open until 9:00 PM most nights and midnight on Friday–Saturday. East metro favorite.
