# Top Bowling Alleys in Miami, FL

From Bird Road to downtown Miami, Doral, and the greater South Florida area, the Magic City and its suburbs offer a solid mix of large centers, boutique lanes, and family spots. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around Miami.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[Miami City Guide](/best-bowling-in-miami)**.

## 1. [Bird Bowl Bowling Center](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/vP6Gi1cxhgQrD8HgrhSz)

**Location**: 9270 SW 40th St, Miami, FL 33165  
**Lanes**: 60  
**Rating**: 4.4/5 (3,865 reviews)  
**Phone**: (305) 221-1221  
**Website**: [birdbowl.com](https://birdbowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $5.50/game, $39.95/hour, $5.50 shoe rental

Sixty-lane center on Bird Road with automatic scoring, nightly specials, and competitive hourly rates. Year-round leagues, summer camps, 8 billiard tables, video arcade, and the Lounge. [Promotions](https://birdbowl.com/promotions/), [reserve online](https://secure.meriq.com/birdbowl/index.asp?go=bowling). Open until midnight most nights and 2:00 AM on Friday–Saturday. Miami’s go-to for lane count and value.
