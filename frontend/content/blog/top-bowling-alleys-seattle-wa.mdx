# Top Bowling Alleys in Seattle, WA

Seattle and the Puget Sound area offer a mix of classic alleys, downtown nightlife bowling, and large centers from Federal Way to Lynnwood. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around Seattle.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[Seattle City Guide](/best-bowling-in-seattle)**.

## 1. [West Seattle Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/ki1cQvcv0FG8VhTci43T)

**Location**: 4505 39th Ave SW, Seattle, WA 98116  
**Lanes**: 32  
**Rating**: 4.6/5 (1,240 reviews)  
**Phone**: (206) 932-3731  
**Website**: [wsbowl.com](https://www.wsbowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $46/hour, $5.50 shoe rental

Veteran bowling alley since 1948 with 32 lanes, tournaments, and a restaurant serving burgers and pizza. Pro shop, food, bar, parties, lounge, and online reservations. Open until midnight most nights and 11:00 PM Sunday. West Seattle.
