# Top Bowling Alleys in New York, NY

Whether you're in Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, or just across the river in New Jersey, the New York area offers a wide range of bowling—from music venues with lanes to 50-lane centers and old-school houses. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around New York City.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[New York City Guide](/best-bowling-in-new-york)**.

## 1. [Humdingers](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/rkSweL8UTw2Raby9R1Mk)

**Location**: 64 E Midland Ave, Paramus, NJ 07652  
**Lanes**: 12  
**Rating**: 4.7/5 (4,099 reviews)  
**Phone**: (201) 701-1900  
**Website**: [humdingersnj.com](http://www.humdingersnj.com/)  
**Pricing**: $70/hour

Boutique bowling with 12 SPARK lanes (Brunswick on-lane graphics and games). Laser tag, batting cages, arcade, food, and bar. Open until 10:00 PM most nights and midnight on Saturday. A Bergen County favorite for families and groups.

## 2. [Maple Lanes RVC](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/4DoasvTw60hBQp8Wsd89)

**Location**: 100 Maple Ave, Rockville Centre, NY 11570  
**Rating**: 4.6/5 (812 reviews)  
**Phone**: (516) 678-3010  
**Website**: [rvclanes.com](http://www.rvclanes.com/)

Open bowling 9:00 AM–midnight daily. Cosmic bowling, leagues, tournaments, event space, parties, food, bar, arcade, and bowling specials. [Leagues](https://maplelanes.com/rvc/leagues/), [tournaments](https://maplelanes.com/rvc/tournaments/). Long Island staple.

## 3. [Brooklyn Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/xjeWO4pZPOlpKaimmpRX)

**Location**: 61 Wythe Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11249  
**Lanes**: 16  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (3,887 reviews)  
**Phone**: (718) 963-3369  
**Website**: [brooklynbowl.com/brooklyn](http://www.brooklynbowl.com/brooklyn)  
**Pricing**: $30/lane per half hour (Mon–Thu), $35 (Fri–Sun), up to 8 guests per lane; $5.95 shoe rental  
**Price Level**: $$ (Moderate)

Music venue first, with 16 bowling lanes, Blue Ribbon food, and a full bar. Leather couches, lane-side service, and a view of the stage. Reserve for bowling or catch a show—Williamsburg’s must-do bowling experience.

## 4. [Rab's Country Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/kx6dcbjEAQJqu69XVBe7)

**Phone**: (718) 979-1600  
**Website**: [bowlatrabs.com](http://www.bowlatrabs.com/)

48 lanes, cosmic bowling (LED light show, black lights, music videos), arcade, bar, leagues, and parties. Open until midnight most nights and 2:00 AM on Friday–Saturday. Staten Island’s go-to for open bowling and late-night glow.

## Planning Your Visit

Each venue offers something different:

- **Humdingers**: Best rating (4.7) and review count in NJ; boutique SPARK lanes, laser tag, batting cages
- **Maple Lanes RVC**: Long Island; cosmic, leagues, tournaments, open until midnight daily
- **Brooklyn Bowl**: Best for music + bowling; 16 lanes, Blue Ribbon food, reserve for shows or lanes
- **Rab's Country Lanes**: Most lanes (48) in NYC proper; Staten Island, cosmic until 2 AM weekends
- **Bowlero Belleville**: North Jersey; 32 lanes, reserve online, cosmic, sports bar
- **Lucky Strike Mineola**: Long Island; 40 lanes, upscale, cosmic, reserve online
- **Laser Bounce Queens**: Family fun; bowling + laser tag + VR at Atlas Park
- **Jersey Lanes**: Best value ($15/hr); 42 lanes, Linden NJ, leagues, solar-powered
- **Homefield Bowl**: Old-school vibe; Yonkers, wood-paneled bar, leagues, parties
- **Lucky Strike Times Square**: Only 50-lane center in Manhattan; $$$, reserve online, open late
