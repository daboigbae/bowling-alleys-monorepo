# Top Bowling Alleys in San Francisco, CA

Whether you're planning a league night, a party in the Presidio, or a casual roll after work, the San Francisco area offers a mix of boutique lanes and full-size centers. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around the City by the Bay.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[San Francisco City Guide](/best-bowling-in-san-francisco)**.

## 1. [Mission Bowling Club](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/4H78g5bfZ6p3S3U18oLD)

**Location**: 3176 17th St, San Francisco, CA 94110  
**Lanes**: 6  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (652 reviews)  
**Phone**: (415) 863-2695  
**Website**: [missionbowlingclub.com](http://missionbowlingclub.com/)

Retro bowling alley with 6 lanes, a full bar, and an attached restaurant serving upscale comfort fare. Leagues, pro shop, and event space. Closed Mondays; open late Friday and Saturday. A Mission District favorite.

## 2. [Fifth Arrow](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/caj7MPicyWnRYzff0H18)

**Location**: 430 Mason St, San Francisco, CA 94102  
**Lanes**: 3  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (122 reviews)  
**Phone**: (415) 872-5744  
**Website**: [fiftharrow.com](http://www.fiftharrow.com/)  
**Price Level**: $$ (Moderate)

Industrial-chic spot with a 3-lane bowling alley, retro games, New American fare, and cocktails. Bar, food, parties, and arcade. Compact and stylish—ideal for a unique night near Union Square.

## 3. [Bel Mateo Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/kIcLr3AR8FjPzgqeomvK)

**Location**: 4330 Olympic Ave, San Mateo, CA 94403  
**Rating**: 4.4/5 (1,205 reviews)  
**Phone**: (650) 341-2616  
**Website**: [belmateobowl.com](http://belmateobowl.com/)

Bowling with dozens of lanes plus arcade games, a snack bar, and a cocktail lounge. Leagues, parties, pro shop, bowling specials, and air hockey. One of the Peninsula’s most popular centers with long hours and weekend availability.

## 4. [Classic Bowling Center](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/YdiUc8VeGiunZxxYXem5)

**Location**: 900 King Dr, Daly City, CA 94015  
**Lanes**: 60  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (760 reviews)  
**Phone**: (650) 878-0300  
**Website**: [classicbowling.com](http://www.classicbowling.com/)  
**Pricing**: $55/hour, $5 shoe rental

Full-service bowling complex with 60 lanes, cafe, pro shop, arcade, and sports bar/lounge. Cosmic and glow bowling, leagues, and parties. Open until 1:00 AM on weekends. [Reserve lanes](https://www.mybowlingpassport.com/704/6770/book) online.

## 5. [Presidio Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/KEJRuMj64xuznPBWKfha)

**Location**: 93 Moraga Ave, San Francisco, CA 94129  
**Lanes**: 12  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (421 reviews)  
**Phone**: (415) 561-2695  
**Website**: [presidiobowl.com](http://www.presidiobowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $85/hour, $7.50 shoe rental

A 12-lane center in the heart of the Presidio National Park with Qubica scoring, bumpers for kids, a full bar and grill (45+ beers/ciders/seltzers, 19 wines), and cosmic bowling. Perfect for parties, corporate events, and league play.

## 6. [Lucky Strike San Francisco](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/va3BQIoMIiOMzOFKNjU6)

**Location**: 200 King St, San Francisco, CA 94107  
**Lanes**: 12  
**Rating**: 4.2/5 (1,165 reviews)  
**Phone**: (415) 400-8260  
**Website**: [luckystrikeent.com](https://www.luckystrikeent.com/location/lucky-strike-san-francisco)  
**Pricing**: ~$112/hour, ~$14 shoe rental  
**Price Level**: $$$ (Upscale)

Upscale entertainment with bowling, craft food and cocktails, and a lively arcade. Private lanes, lounge, billiards, air hockey, cosmic bowling, leagues, and parties. Near SoMa and the ballpark—reservations recommended.

## 7. [Hometown Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/TiPuFOlu8hVECzzAwwM5)

**Location**: 237 El Camino Real, South San Francisco, CA 94080  
**Rating**: 4.1/5 (132 reviews)  
**Phone**: (650) 763-1643  
**Website**: [hometownbowl.com](https://www.hometownbowl.com/)

Neighborhood bowling with food, bar, parties, billiards, and bowling specials. Open Thursday through Sunday—check hours before you go. A solid option south of the city.

## 8. [AMF Pinole Valley Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/GwvRG0UhtbIhtTAnyHiB)

**Location**: 1580 Pinole Valley Rd, Pinole, CA 94564  
**Rating**: 4.1/5 (1,003 reviews)  
**Phone**: (510) 724-9130  
**Website**: [amf.com](https://www.amf.com/location/amf-pinole-valley-lanes/)  
**Pricing**: ~$60/hour, ~$12 shoe rental

Long-standing chain spot with casual grub, pitchers, leagues, and party events. Arcade, billiards, pro shop, wheelchair accessible. Open late on weekends until midnight. East Bay value.

## 9. [Round1 Bowling & Arcade Southland Mall](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/jMlGA7gTzYqcox2kdSNo)

**Location**: 551 Southland Mall, Hayward, CA 94545  
**Rating**: 4.1/5 (1,278 reviews)  
**Phone**: (510) 300-6677  
**Website**: [round1usa.com](http://round1usa.com/)

Bowling plus arcade, karaoke, billiards, ping pong, darts, cosmic bowling, and food. Open until 2:00 AM every day—ideal for late-night fun in Hayward. Kid-friendly and party-friendly.

## 10. [Bowlero Alameda](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/72Kb6U48Aoo0ewwVNLkd)

**Location**: 300 Park St, Alameda, CA 94501  
**Lanes**: 40  
**Rating**: 4.1/5 (1,565 reviews)  
**Phone**: (510) 523-6767  
**Website**: [bowlero.com](https://www.bowlero.com/location/bowlero-alameda/)  
**Pricing**: ~$63/hour, ~$12 shoe rental

40 lanes, arcade, billiards, and one of the top sports bars in Alameda. Family-friendly, wheelchair accessible, with online lane reservations. Open until midnight most nights and 1:00 AM on weekends.

## Planning Your Visit

Each venue offers something different:

- **Mission Bowling Club**: Best for atmosphere and food in the Mission; 6 lanes, leagues, events
- **Fifth Arrow**: Unique 3-lane experience near Union Square; industrial-chic, $$ 
- **Bel Mateo Bowl**: Most reviews on the Peninsula; dozens of lanes, arcade, leagues
- **Classic Bowling Center**: Most lanes (60) in the area; cosmic, pro shop, late hours
- **Presidio Bowl**: Only bowling in the Presidio; 12 lanes, bar & grill, parties
- **Lucky Strike SF**: Best for upscale SoMa/King St; private lanes, craft cocktails, $$$
- **Hometown Bowl**: South San Francisco neighborhood spot; Thu–Sun only
- **AMF Pinole Valley**: East Bay chain value; leagues, parties, late weekends
- **Round1 Southland**: Latest hours (until 2 AM); bowling + arcade + karaoke in Hayward
- **Bowlero Alameda**: 40 lanes on the island; sports bar, arcade, online reservations

Whether you want a boutique 6-lane club, a 60-lane center, or cosmic bowling in the Presidio, the San Francisco area has a lane to match. For full details, pricing, and hours, use our **[San Francisco City Guide](/best-bowling-in-san-francisco)**.
