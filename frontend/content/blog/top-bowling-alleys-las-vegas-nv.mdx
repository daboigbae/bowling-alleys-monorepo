# Top Bowling Alleys in Las Vegas, NV

Whether you're escaping the casino floor, planning a family outing, or looking for cosmic bowling and league action, the Las Vegas area delivers some of the biggest and best bowling centers in the country. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around Las Vegas.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling schedules, visit our **[Las Vegas City Guide](/best-bowling-in-las-vegas)**.

## 1. [Red Rock Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/IzNCoBRJwby7CqXyUYpe)

**Location**: 11011 W Charleston Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89135  
**Lanes**: 72  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (1,296 reviews)  
**Phone**: (702) 797-7777  
**Website**: [redrockresort.com](https://www.redrockresort.com/see-and-do/red-rock-lanes/)

With 72 luxury lanes, a stunning lounge, lively game room, private VIP suites, and a snack bar, the lanes at Red Rock are right up your alley. Cosmic and glow bowling, bar, food, parties, and leagues. Open until midnight most nights and until 2:00 AM on Friday and Saturday.

## Planning Your Visit

Each venue offers something different:

- **Red Rock Lanes**: Most lanes (72), luxury resort vibe, cosmic bowling, VIP suites
- **South Point**: Best hours (7 AM–2 AM daily), per-game pricing, 64 lanes
- **Sam's Town**: Strong league and family scene, 56 lanes, pro shop
- **Orleans**: 52 lanes, lockers, solid late hours
- **Gold Coast**: Premier league and junior programs, central location
- **Velocity Esports**: Bowling + esports and arcade; good for mixed groups
- **Brooklyn Bowl**: Best for music + food + bowling; opens 5 PM
- **Suncoast**: "Best of Las Vegas" winner, 64 lanes, full amenities
- **Santa Fe Lanes**: 60 lanes, Johnny Rockets, north valley
- **Round1**: Latest hours (until 2 AM), bowling + arcade + karaoke

Whether you want resort luxury, 24/7-style hours, league play, or cosmic bowling after dark, Las Vegas has a center to match. For full details, pricing, and cosmic schedules, use our **[Las Vegas City Guide](/best-bowling-in-las-vegas)**.
