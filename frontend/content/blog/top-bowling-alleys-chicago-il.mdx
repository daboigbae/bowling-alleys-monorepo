# Top Bowling Alleys in Chicago, IL

From the North Side to the suburbs, Chicago and Chicagoland offer a strong mix of landmark alleys, Bowlero centers, and neighborhood houses. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around the Windy City.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[Chicago City Guide](/best-bowling-in-chicago)**.

## 1. [Stardust Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/sjfUr8j5vMzWD8soIWDL)

**Location**: 37 E Lorraine Ave, Addison, IL 60101  
**Lanes**: 84  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (1,350 reviews)  
**Phone**: (630) 833-1040  
**Website**: [stardust.bowlbowlbowl.com](https://stardust.bowlbowlbowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: ~$40/hour, ~$5.69 shoe rental

Long-running bowling alley with 84 lanes, 4 lounges, live music, games, TV sports, and a diner. Parties, leagues, food, pro shop, arcade, and bar. [Specials](https://stardust.bowlbowlbowl.com/specials/). Open until 11:00 PM most nights and 1:00 AM Thursday–Saturday. One of the largest centers in the area.

## Planning Your Visit

Each venue offers something different:

- **Stardust Bowl**: Most lanes (84) in the list; 4 lounges, live music, diner, Addison
- **Beverly Lanes**: Family-focused; 24 bumper lanes, lessons, pro shop, closed Mon
- **Bowlero Mount Prospect**: 36 lanes, cosmic, reserve online, Mt Prospect
- **Bowlero Roselle**: 32 lanes + mini golf, cosmic, reserve online, Roselle
- **Arlington Lanes**: Per-game pricing ($7), The Hideout Pub, Arlington Heights
- **Waveland Bowl**: Best Chicago proper landmark; cosmic, call ahead, North Side
- **Bowlero Buffalo Grove**: 32 lanes, laser tag, cosmic, reserve online, Buffalo Grove
- **Bowlero Woodridge**: 40 lanes, cosmic, reserve online, southwest suburbs
- **Kings Lincoln Park**: Best in-city vibe; 16 lanes, food & cocktails, $$
- **Classic Bowl**: Open until 1 AM daily; Morton Grove, tournaments

Whether you want 84 lanes in Addison, Waveland on the North Side, or Kings in Lincoln Park, Chicago and Chicagoland have a lane to match. For full details, pricing, and hours, use our **[Chicago City Guide](/best-bowling-in-chicago)**.
