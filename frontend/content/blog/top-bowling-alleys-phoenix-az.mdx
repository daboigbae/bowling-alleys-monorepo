# Top Bowling Alleys in Phoenix, AZ

Whether you're escaping the heat, planning a league night, or looking for duckpin bowling and axe throwing, the Phoenix metro area delivers a strong mix of entertainment centers and traditional lanes. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around the Valley of the Sun.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[Phoenix City Guide](/best-bowling-in-phoenix)**.

- **House Rules**: Only duckpin bowling in the list; axe throwing, upscale cocktails, Scottsdale
- **AMF Tempe Village**: 32 lanes, cosmic bowling, leagues, classic chain vibe
- **Mavrix**: Scottsdale; 22 lanes, laser tag, arcade, lower hourly rate, $$
- **Let It Roll**: North Phoenix; Mexican restaurant, patio, cosmic and glow
- **Lucky Strike North Scottsdale**: Most lanes (36) in the list; arcade, sports bar, reserve online
