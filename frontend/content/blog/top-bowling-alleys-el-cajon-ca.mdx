# Top Bowling Alleys in El Cajon, CA

El Cajon and the greater San Diego area offer strong bowling options—from East County’s biggest house (Parkway Bowl) to campus lanes, casino bowling, and downtown spots. Here are the top bowling alleys in and around El Cajon.

For a complete directory with pricing, hours, and cosmic bowling details, visit our **[El Cajon City Guide](/best-bowling-alleys-in-el-cajon)**.

## 1. [Viejas Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/N3WjzQr2Ps79qoJLJrst)

**Location**: 5005 Willows Rd, Alpine, CA 91901  
**Rating**: 4.6/5 (444 reviews)  
**Phone**: (877) 303-2695  
**Website**: [viejas.com](https://viejas.com/bowling-at-viejas/)  
**Pricing**: $5/game, $30/hour, $4 shoe rental

Bowling at Viejas with all-American menu, hi-def sports screens, and arcade games. **Galactic Bowl** on Friday and Saturday nights. Groups up to 10 (same household). Open until 9:00 PM most days and 10:00 PM on Friday–Saturday. East County casino bowling.

## 2. [Aztec Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/weWFUV857mhUbA1QARNE)

**Location**: 6075 Aztec Circle Dr, San Diego, CA 92182  
**Rating**: 4.6/5 (293 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 594-2695  
**Website**: [azteclanes.sdsu.edu](http://azteclanes.sdsu.edu/)  
**Pricing**: $5/game, $3 shoe rental

Campus bowling at SDSU with billiards, ping pong, air hockey, leagues, and parties. [Reserve online](https://azteclanes.sdsu.edu/reserve#form). Open until midnight Thursday–Saturday. A favorite for students and the community near San Diego.

## 3. [MCRD Bowling Alley](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/Um9ss6WvG4MWbcvMY2i1)

**Location**: 3800 Chosin Ave, San Diego, CA 92140  
**Rating**: 4.5/5 (197 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 524-4446  
**Website**: [sandiego.usmc-mccs.org](https://sandiego.usmc-mccs.org/dining-entertainment/the-community-center)

Military bowling at Marine Corps Recruit Depot with leagues, parties, karaoke, billiards, shuffleboard, foosball, and bowling specials. Open to authorized personnel; hours vary (e.g. Mon–Tue 9 AM–9 PM, Thu–Sat until 11 PM, Sun 10 AM–5 PM).

## 4. [San Diego Lawn Bowling Club](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/otHdQzJlY0AeQnJfKwcO)

**Location**: Balboa Dr, San Diego, CA 92101  
**Rating**: 4.6/5 (20 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 238-5457  
**Website**: [sandiegolawnbowling.org](http://www.sandiegolawnbowling.org/)

**Lawn bowling** (outdoor on grass) in Balboa Park—memberships for social play and tournaments, plus lessons. A different sport from tenpin but a San Diego institution.

## 5. [Lucky Strike Chula Vista](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/oyK12x6NpdlN4EQAATeI)

**Location**: 845 Lazo Ct, Chula Vista, CA 91910  
**Lanes**: 48  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (2,364 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 421-4801  
**Website**: [luckystrikeent.com](https://www.luckystrikeent.com/location/lucky-strike-chula-vista)  
**Pricing**: ~$63/hour, ~$13 shoe rental

48 lanes, arcade, billiards, sports bar, lounge, free WiFi, and online lane reservations. Open until midnight most nights and 1:00 AM on Friday–Saturday. South Bay’s largest bowling and entertainment center.

## 6. [Punch Bowl Social](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/e8RDLupXCT6Dxd89GVqZ)

**Location**: 1485 E St, San Diego, CA 92101  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (2,156 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 452-3352  
**Website**: [punchbowlsocial.com](https://punchbowlsocial.com/location/san-diego/)  
**Pricing**: $25/hour  
**Price Level**: $$ (Moderate)

Historic Coliseum Athletic Club in Makers Quarter: bowling, shuffleboard, karaoke, bocce, scratch-made food, and craft cocktails at the 360 bar. Open until 2:00 AM on Friday–Saturday. Downtown San Diego.

## 7. [Parkway Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/bGgSaGzGIHKfAmSCFRTO)

**Location**: 1280 Fletcher Pkwy, El Cajon, CA 92020  
**Lanes**: 68  
**Rating**: 4.3/5 (1,708 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 448-4111  
**Website**: [parkwaybowl.com](https://parkwaybowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: From $3.50–$4.25/game (juniors/seniors/adults), $19.95–$43.95/hour per lane, $5 shoe rental

El Cajon’s flagship: 68 lanes, laser tag, grill, billiards. Leagues, parties, [group packages](https://parkwaybowl.com/group-packages/), [reservations](https://reservations.meriq.com/parkwaybowl/). Open until 11:30 PM most nights and midnight on Friday–Saturday. East County’s best value and lane count.

## 8. [East Village Tavern+Bowl](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/3t9WvOeqO7tS0eMceZTy)

**Location**: 930 Market St, San Diego, CA 92101  
**Lanes**: 12  
**Rating**: 4.2/5 (719 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 677-2695  
**Website**: [tavernbowl.com](http://tavernbowl.com/)  
**Pricing**: $5/game, $55/hour, $5 shoe rental  
**Price Level**: $$ (Moderate)

Downtown East Village: up to 12 lanes, HD screens, full menu (chicken sandwiches, tacos, salads, appetizers). Open late seven days a week—ideal for corporate events, parties, and casual bowling.

## 9. [Boardwalk](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/UfP6tuchxEnFSeqYkkVI)

**Location**: 1280 Fletcher Pkwy, El Cajon, CA 92020  
**Rating**: 4.2/5 (819 reviews)  
**Phone**: (619) 449-7805  
**Website**: [boardwalk-parkway.com](http://www.boardwalk-parkway.com/)

Entertainment complex with multiple arcades, rides, laser tag, Krazy Karz, pizzeria, and a **neighboring bowling alley** (Parkway Bowl) in the same complex. Leagues and parties. Same campus as Parkway Bowl—perfect for families who want bowling plus arcade and attractions.

## 10. [Mira Mesa Lanes](https://bowlingalleys.io/venue/26mQIiVoeeIRj0YBh2z7)

**Location**: 8210 Mira Mesa Blvd, San Diego, CA 92126  
**Lanes**: 20  
**Rating**: 4.2/5 (985 reviews)  
**Phone**: (858) 578-0500  
**Website**: [miramesalanes.com](http://miramesalanes.com/)  
**Pricing**: $50/hour, $6 shoe rental

20 lanes, cosmic and glow bowling, arcade, bar, food, billiards, and one of the nation’s largest youth bowling programs (scholarships, tournaments). [Reserve lanes](https://miramesalanes.reservewithrex.com/select-location?venue=Mira%20Mesa&date=2026-01-01&guests=1) online. Serving San Diego for decades.

- **Aztec Lanes**: Best campus option; SDSU, reserve online, billiards & games
- **MCRD Bowling Alley**: Military facility; authorized personnel only
- **San Diego Lawn Bowling Club**: Lawn bowling in Balboa Park; memberships & lessons
- **Lucky Strike Chula Vista**: Most lanes (48) in the list; south bay, reserve online
- **Punch Bowl Social**: Downtown; bowling + shuffleboard + karaoke, $$
- **Parkway Bowl**: El Cajon anchor; 68 lanes, laser tag, grill, best value
- **East Village Tavern+Bowl**: Downtown; 12 lanes, full menu, open late
- **Boardwalk**: El Cajon; arcade, rides, laser tag, pizzeria + bowling next door at Parkway
- **Mira Mesa Lanes**: Strong youth and league scene; cosmic bowling, reserve online
